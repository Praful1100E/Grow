# Team Structure for TechTestHub

## Overview
TechTestHub will be founded by a 4-member team, with each member holding equal equity (25% each). The team is designed to cover all essential aspects of the business, from technical development to business operations and marketing.

## Team Roles and Responsibilities

### 1. Website Development
- **Role**: Lead web developer.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Design and develop the company website.
  - Ensure responsive design and user-friendly interface.
  - Integrate payment systems and user authentication.
- **Skills Required**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, frameworks like React or Vue.js.

### 2. Payment Transactions
- **Role**: Payment systems specialist.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Set up and manage payment gateways (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).
  - Ensure secure and compliant transactions.
  - Handle refunds, disputes, and financial reporting.
- **Skills Required**: Knowledge of payment APIs, financial regulations, basic accounting.

### 3. App Development
- **Role**: Mobile app developer.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Develop mobile applications for iOS and Android.
  - Ensure app functionality mirrors web platform.
  - Handle app store submissions and updates.
- **Skills Required**: React Native, Flutter, or native development (Swift/Kotlin).

### 4. Server Handling
- **Role**: DevOps and backend engineer.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Manage servers and cloud infrastructure (e.g., AWS, Azure).
  - Ensure scalability, security, and uptime.
  - Develop backend APIs and database management.
- **Skills Required**: Cloud platforms, Docker, Node.js/Python for backend.

### 5. University/College Outreach
- **Role**: Business development and partnerships.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Contact universities and colleges for partnerships.
  - Organize events and collaborations.
  - Build relationships with educational institutions.
- **Skills Required**: Sales, networking, communication.

### 6. Promotions and Marketing
- **Role**: Marketing and promotions lead.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Develop marketing strategies and campaigns.
  - Manage social media, content creation, and advertising.
  - Analyze market trends and user feedback.
- **Skills Required**: Digital marketing, SEO, content creation.

## Equity Distribution
- Each of the 4 members: 25% equity.
- Equity vesting schedule: 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff to ensure commitment.

## Hiring Process
- Identify candidates with relevant skills and passion for education.
- Conduct interviews focusing on technical skills, business acumen, and cultural fit.
- Offer competitive salaries with equity incentives.

## Future Expansion
- As the company grows, hire additional staff for specialized roles.
- Consider remote work options to attract global talent.
